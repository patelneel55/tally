THIS CERTIFIES THAT _________________________ is the registered holder of _____ fully paid and non-assessable shares of the above-named Preferred Stock of the par value of $1.00 each transferable on the books of JPMorgan Chase & Co. by the holder hereof in person or by duly authorized attorney upon surrender of this certificate properly endorsed.

Each share of Series OO Preferred Stock shall be identical in all respects to every other share of Series OO Preferred Stock. The Series OO Preferred Stock is perpetual and has no maturity date.

Dividends on the Series OO Preferred Stock shall not be mandatory. Holders of Series OO Preferred Stock shall be entitled to receive, when, as, and if declared by the Board of Directors of JPMorgan Chase & Co. or any duly authorized committee of the Board of Directors, out of funds legally available therefor, non-cumulative cash dividends at the rate determined as set forth in the Certificate of Designations, payable quarterly in arrears on the 1st day of March, June, September and December of each year.

The Series OO Preferred Stock shall not be redeemable prior to March 1, 2030. On and after that date, the Series OO Preferred Stock shall be redeemable at the option of JPMorgan Chase & Co., in whole or in part, at a redemption price equal to $25,000 per share of Series OO Preferred Stock, plus any declared and unpaid dividends, without accumulation of any undeclared dividends.

In the event of any liquidation, dissolution or winding up of JPMorgan Chase & Co., the holders of Series OO Preferred Stock shall be entitled to receive a liquidation preference of $25,000 per share, plus any declared and unpaid dividends, without accumulation of any undeclared dividends, before any distribution is made to the holders of Common Stock.

The Series OO Preferred Stock shall not have any voting rights except as set forth in the Certificate of Designations or as otherwise required by law.

Reference is made to the further provisions of the Series OO Preferred Stock set forth in the Certificate of Designations, a copy of which shall be furnished without charge to any stockholder who makes a written request therefor to the Secretary of JPMorgan Chase & Co.
